Kiosk Verification Modes

Multi-Pose Averaging

When enabled, the system verifies against all 5 enrolled poses (Front, L, R, Up, Down) rather than just the front reference. Slightly slower but much more accurate.

Active Liveness Challenge

Force students to perform a random action (e.g. "Blink twice", "Turn head left") before validation to prevent printed photo or tablet spoofing.

Offline Sync Mode

Store encrypted facial encodings directly in the browser's IndexedDB. Allows the kiosk to operate without internet and sync later.
